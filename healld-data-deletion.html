Data Deletion Instructions

You can delete your Healld account and all associated data in two ways:

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

Open the Healld app, go to Profile → Settings → Account & Data → Delete Account. This will permanently delete your account and all associated data from our servers, including:

  • Your user profile (email, name, avatar)
  • Mood check-ins and emotion history
  • Journal entries and gratitude notes
  • Self-assessment responses and results
  • Habit logs and sleep schedules
  • Breathwork and mindfulness session activity
  • Community posts, replies, and reactions

Option 2: Email Request

If you are unable to access the app, please email mobilebytesensei@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Healld Account" and include the email address associated with your account.

Your account and all associated data will be deleted from our servers within 7 days of receiving your request.

What happens after deletion?

Once your account is deleted, all your personal data — including your wellbeing data — is permanently removed or irreversibly anonymised from our servers (powered by Supabase). This action cannot be undone. Aggregated, anonymous analytics data that can no longer be traced back to you will not be deleted. Limited records may be retained only where required to meet legal obligations.


For more detail on how your data is handled, see the Healld Privacy Policy.
