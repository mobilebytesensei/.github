Data Deletion Instructions

You can delete your Mood Movies account and all associated data in two ways:

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

Open the Mood Movies app, go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This will immediately and permanently delete your account and all associated data from our servers, including:

  • Your user profile (email, name, avatar)
  • Mood and emotion history
  • Watchlist and favorites
  • Genre preferences and top picks
  • Search history
  • Mood journal entries
  • Streaks and achievements

Option 2: Email Request

If you are unable to access the app, please email mobilebytesensei@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Mood Movies Account" and include the email address associated with your account.

Your account and all associated data will be deleted from our servers within 7 days of receiving your request.

What happens after deletion?

Once your account is deleted, all your personal data is permanently removed from our servers (Supabase). This action cannot be undone. Anonymous analytics data that has already been aggregated cannot be traced back to you and will not be deleted.
