# Privacy Policy for Monetization Sample

**Last Updated:** December 30, 2024

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Monetization Sample app ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application. This app is designed as a demonstration of various monetization strategies and serves educational purposes.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
We do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you explicitly provide it through the app's features.

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

#### Device Information
- Device model and manufacturer
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- Screen size and density
- Device language settings

#### App Usage Information
- App features accessed
- Time spent in the app
- In-app purchase history
- Crash reports and performance data
- Session duration and frequency

#### Location Information
We may collect approximate location data (country/city level) based on your IP address to serve relevant advertisements and comply with regional regulations.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Monetization Purposes
- **Advertising:** Display personalized or non-personalized advertisements through Google AdMob and other ad networks
- **In-App Purchases:** Process and validate purchases made through Google Play Billing
- **Analytics:** Understand app usage patterns and improve user experience

### App Functionality
- Provide and maintain app features
- Respond to user inquiries and support requests
- Debug and fix technical issues
- Improve app performance and stability

### Legal Compliance
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Enforce our terms of service
- Protect against fraudulent or illegal activity

## Third-Party Services

This app integrates the following third-party services that may collect information:

### Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

**Data Collected by AdMob:**

### Google Play Services
For in-app purchases and app functionality, we use:
- **Google Play Billing:** Processes in-app purchases
- **Google Play Core:** Handles app updates and feature delivery

### Firebase Analytics (if applicable)
We may use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage:
- User engagement metrics

- Custom events and conversions

For more information, see [Firebase Privacy Information](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

By default, the app may show personalized ads based on your interests and app usage. You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

### Advertising ID
We use your device's Advertising ID to serve relevant ads. This identifier is:

- Used solely for advertising purposes

- Each network may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies

If you are located in the EEA, you have additional rights under GDPR:
- Right to access your personal data
- Right to rectification of inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

**Legal Basis for Processing:**
- Consent for personalized advertising
- Legitimate interests for app functionality and improvement
- Legal obligations for compliance with laws

### California - CCPA/CPRA
If you are a California resident, you have rights under CCPA:
- Right to know what information is collected
- Right to know if information is sold or shared
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
- Right to deletion of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination

**Note:** We do not "sell" personal information as defined by CCPA.

If you are in Brazil, you have rights under LGPD similar to GDPR, including the right to access, correct, delete, and port your data.
