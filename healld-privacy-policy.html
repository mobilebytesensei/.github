Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Healld app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by MobileByteSensei (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS". Healld is a mental-wellness application, so much of the information you choose to provide is sensitive personal data concerning your health and emotional wellbeing, and the Service Provider treats it accordingly.


Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and the time spent on those pages
  • The operating system and app version you use on your mobile device
  • A pseudonymous installation identifier used for diagnostics

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.


Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, the Application collects your email address and authentication credentials. This information is used to create and manage your user profile and to restore your data across devices.


Health & Wellbeing Data (Sensitive)

To provide its core features, the Application collects the wellbeing information you choose to record, including:

  • Mood check-ins and emotion selections
  • Journal entries and gratitude notes
  • Self-assessment responses and results
  • Habit logs, sleep schedules, and breathwork / mindfulness session activity
  • Community posts, replies, and reactions you choose to share

This data is stored on our servers (powered by Supabase) and is associated with your account to provide your personal insights and progress views and to restore your data across devices. The Service Provider does not sell your personal data and does not use your wellbeing data for advertising. Community content you post is visible to other users — please do not share anything you consider private.


Legal Basis (India — Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023)

The Service Provider processes your personal data on the basis of the consent you provide when you sign up and use the Application, and only for the purposes described in this policy. You may withdraw your consent at any time (see Opt-Out Rights and Account Deletion). Where you are located in a jurisdiction with additional data-protection rights (e.g. GDPR/UK GDPR, CCPA), those rights also apply to your data.


Third Party Access

Only the data needed to operate the Application is processed by the Service Provider's trusted service providers. The Application utilizes third-party services that have their own Privacy Policy about handling data. Below are links to the Privacy Policy of the third-party service providers used by the Application:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Firebase (Crashlytics, Analytics, App Distribution)
  • Supabase (database, authentication, storage)

The Application may generate wellbeing insights using an AI service; where feasible, only de-identified or aggregated inputs are used, and no community identity is shared for advertising. The Service Provider may disclose information: as required by law (e.g. to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process); when they believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect their rights, your safety or the safety of others, or to investigate fraud; and with trusted service providers who work on their behalf under contractual confidentiality obligations.


Healld is Not a Medical Service

The Application supports self-reflection and wellbeing habits. It is not a medical device, diagnosis, or treatment, and it is not a crisis or emergency service. If you are in distress or danger, contact your local emergency services or a qualified professional immediately.


Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling it. You may also access, review, correct, and export your data, and delete individual entries, from within the Application.


Data Retention Policy

The Service Provider will retain your data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable time thereafter. If you delete specific entries they are removed from active systems; backups are purged on a rolling cycle. If you would like the Service Provider to delete data you have provided, you may delete your account in-app or contact mobilebytesensei@gmail.com and they will respond in a reasonable time.


Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the Application through the account / privacy settings. Upon account deletion, your personal data — including mood history, journal entries, assessments, habits, sleep data, and preferences — will be permanently removed or irreversibly anonymised from our servers, except where limited records must be retained to meet legal obligations. This action cannot be undone. See also the Healld data deletion instructions.


Children

The Application is not intended for anyone under the age of 18, and the Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a minor has provided personal information, please contact mobilebytesensei@gmail.com so it can be deleted.


Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and stored securely on Supabase infrastructure with row-level security policies ensuring users can only access their own data. The Service Provider will notify you and the relevant authority of a personal-data breach where required by law.


Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes by updating this page, and for material changes will provide an in-app notice. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly; continued use is deemed approval of all changes.


This privacy policy is effective as of June 26, 2026.
